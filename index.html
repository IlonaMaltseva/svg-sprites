SVG Sprites

Create the file spite-icons.svg or something.

It should just be an <svg> tag, with a <defs>> tag (which just means you are defining stuff to use later), and then a bunch of <symbol> tags. Each <g> tag will have a unique ID, and will wrap all the paths and whatnot for each icon.

It's a magic :)

You can used a svg prite generator https://svgsprit.es/
